## What was covered
- Combat sequence: surprise → initiative → turns → rounds
- Initiative formula: d20 + DEX modifier
- Four turn resources: Movement, Action, Bonus Action, Reaction
- Bonus Actions only exist when explicitly granted by a rule
- Attack roll: d20 + ability modifier + proficiency bonus vs AC
- Damage: weapon die + same ability modifier used to hit
- Critical hit: double the damage dice (not the modifier)
- Natural 20 = auto-hit crit; natural 1 = auto-miss
- AC formulas by armor type
- Advantage/Disadvantage: roll 2d20, take higher/lower; any Advantage + any Disadvantage = straight roll
- 0 HP: monsters die; PCs make Death Saving Throws (10+ success, 9- failure; 3 of either = stabilized or dead)
