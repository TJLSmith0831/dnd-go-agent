Ability Scores & Modifiers

The six scores that define every creature in 5e — and the modifier formula behind every check.

The Six Abilities

STR Strength Melee attacks, carrying, Athletics
DEX Dexterity Ranged attacks, AC, Stealth, Acrobatics
CON Constitution Hit points, concentration saves
INT Intelligence Knowledge, Investigation, Arcana
WIS Wisdom Perception, Insight, Survival
CHA Charisma Persuasion, Deception, Performance

Score → Modifier

modifier = ⌊(score − 10) / 2⌋

In Go: int(math.Floor(float64(score-10) / 2)) — not plain integer division, which truncates toward zero.
